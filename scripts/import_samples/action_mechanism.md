# Action Night at the Gallery

Author: Demo Author
Setting: A gallery charity night where player actions can change who reaches the closing vote.

## Public Intro

A sculpture is destroyed during the auction preview. The table can discuss motives, submit actions, and then resolve the night.

## Rules

Players discuss, submit one action card, resolve actions, and then vote on who should answer for the damage.

## Cast

- Nia: A curator who manages the gallery floor. | Secret: Nia moved the display rope. | Goal: Keep the donors calm.
- Omar: A donor who wants the sculpture insured. | Secret: Omar paid for a false appraisal. | Goal: Shift suspicion away from the appraisal.
- Priya: A restorer who knows the material. | Secret: Priya saw a guard leave the side door. | Goal: Prove the break was staged.

## Actions

- GUARD target: protects a target from one destructive action.
- MURDER target: represents removing a candidate from the vote in this toy sample.
- INVESTIGATE target: gives the actor public status information about a target.
- DECLARE text: reads a public declaration before the final vote.
- VOTE target: counts only if the actor can still vote.

## Clues

- Broken Marble Dust: The dust pattern suggests the sculpture was struck from behind.
- Insurance Letter: The insurance policy was changed the same morning.

## Truth

- The sculpture was deliberately broken before the public argument.
- The insurance change gives one possible motive, but action results decide the final resolution in this mock.
