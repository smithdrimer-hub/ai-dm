# Tiny Imported Manor

Author: Demo Author
Setting: A storm keeps four guests inside a quiet manor.

## Public Intro

The host is found unconscious after dinner. Everyone stayed inside the manor, and the DM should keep the first discussion focused on public timelines.

## Rules

Discuss openly, reveal clues when the DM advances phases, then make an accusation before the final reveal.

## Cast

- Ada: A calm botanist who says she checked the greenhouse. | Secret: Ada moved the silver cup before dinner. | Goal: Avoid being blamed for the cup.
- Bruno: A nervous butler who controls the dining room keys. | Secret: Bruno saw Ada near the study. | Goal: Protect the household reputation.
- Cora: A visiting journalist who wants the truth. | Secret: Cora heard a code word in the hallway. | Goal: Publish the correct story.

## Clues

- Tarnished Cup: The silver cup has a bitter almond smell.
- Muddy Key: The dining room key has greenhouse mud on it.

## Truth

- The cup was poisoned before dinner.
- [code: LANTERN] Ada moved the poisoned cup from the study to the dining room.
