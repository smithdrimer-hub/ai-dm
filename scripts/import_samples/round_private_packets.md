# Round Locked Parlor

Author: Demo Author
Setting: A small parlor game where secrets arrive in two rounds.

## Public Intro

The guests gather after a missing necklace is reported. The first round should only use public alibis.

## Rules

Discuss public alibis, then the DM releases each round of private packets before the final accusation.

## Cast

- Iris: A jeweler who appraised the necklace. | Secret: Iris replaced the clasp during the first toast. | Goal: Keep the appraisal believable.
- Jonah: A cousin who says he never entered the study. | Secret: Jonah hid a torn receipt in his coat. | Goal: Avoid attention on the receipt.
- Mira: A pianist who noticed the room order. | Secret: Mira heard the study door click twice. | Goal: Reconstruct the order of movements.

## Round 1 Private Packets

- Iris receives: You saw Jonah near the study before dessert.
- Jonah receives: You found a torn receipt but do not know who dropped it.
- Mira receives: You heard a click before the lights went out.

## Round 2 Private Packets

- Iris receives: The clasp was already loose when you appraised it.
- Jonah receives: The receipt names the repair shop.
- Mira receives: The second click came after the public argument.

## Clues

- Torn Receipt: The receipt mentions a rushed clasp repair.
- Piano Bench Note: A note under the bench lists the dinner order.

## Truth

- The necklace was taken during the blackout.
- The loose clasp explains why the theft looked faster than it was.
